How It Works

From invite to evacuation list in four steps

A complete visitor journey — before they arrive, while they're on site, and when it matters most.

1

Pre-register guests

Staff create visits in seconds. Guests get a branded email with a QR code — no app to install.

2

Scan or tap to check in

Visitors scan their QR at the kiosk camera, or tap Check In. Employees use printed staff QR badges.

3

Hosts notified instantly

Email and SMS alerts reach the right host the moment their guest arrives — photo included.

4

One-tap evacuation list

See and print everyone on site right now — visitors and staff — from any device, any time.

Features

Everything a modern front desk needs

Purpose-built for offices, schools, clinics, warehouses, and multi-site organizations.

Pre-Registration & Invitations

Send branded email invitations with QR codes and visit details. Track status from sent → opened → in building → checked out.

QR Check-In & Staff Badges

Guests scan their invitation QR at the kiosk camera. Employees print ID badges and tap in/out with the same flow.

Photo Capture

Optionally snap a visitor photo at check-in — admin-toggleable per organization. Photos appear in the live visit log.

Instant Host Notifications

Email and SMS alerts powered by SendGrid and Twilio. Hosts know the second their guest arrives.

Evacuation List & Reports

Live on-site roster with one-tap print for roll call. Export full visit history to CSV whenever you need it.

Any Device, Anywhere

The kiosk runs on any tablet, iPad, or touchscreen in the browser. The admin dashboard is fully mobile-responsive.

MFA & Org-Wide Enforcement

Authenticator-app MFA for every user, with an admin switch to require it organization-wide. Role-based access built in.

Offline Kiosk Alerts

The kiosk phones home every minute. If it goes quiet, admins get an automatic email — and another when it recovers.

Multi-Tenant & Teams

Separate organizations, each with their own admins, receptionists, hosts, and settings. Managed centrally by you.

Admin Control

A dashboard your front desk will actually love

Everything happening across your locations, in one clean view — designed for receptionists, powerful enough for security teams.

  • Live dashboard: on-site now, expected today, recent activity
  • Printable evacuation list & one-click CSV export
  • Team management: invite admins & receptionists, reset passwords
  • Per-org settings: photo capture, notifications, MFA enforcement
  • Staff ID badge printing with QR — employees tap in and out
