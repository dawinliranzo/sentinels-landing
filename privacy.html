Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Sentinels Sign-In ("Sentinels Sign-In", "we", "us", "our") is a visitor management service operated by SentinelsIT. This policy explains what information we collect when an organization uses our service and when visitors check in at a location running our kiosk, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — names, work email addresses, and roles of the people an organization adds to its account (admins, receptionists).
  • Visitor information — name, email, phone number, company, visit purpose, host, vehicle plate, and any custom fields an organization configures, collected during check-in.
  • Visit records — check-in/check-out times, badge numbers, and sign-in method.
  • Photos — optional visitor or host photos when an organization enables photo capture.
  • Signatures and documents — where an organization requires an NDA, the signed document text and signature image are stored with the visit.
  • Device and usage data — kiosk device identifiers, heartbeat timestamps, and basic technical logs used to operate and secure the service.

2. How we use information

  • To operate the check-in/check-out experience and maintain visit records for the organization.
  • To notify hosts when their visitor arrives (by email and, if enabled, SMS).
  • To provide evacuation lists, reports, and compliance records to the organization.
  • To secure the service, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

3. SMS / text messaging

Our text message program

Sentinels Sign-In sends transactional text messages only — for example, notifying a host that their visitor has arrived, or an administrator that a kiosk went offline. We do not send marketing texts.

  • Non-sharing: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on visitor activity at the organization (typically a few messages per day at most, only when events you opted into occur).
  • Rates: Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • Opt-out: You can stop SMS at any time by replying STOP to any message, or by disabling SMS notifications in the app settings. After opting out you will receive one confirmation text and no further messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP for help, or contact us at support@sentinelskiosk.com.

4. How information is stored and shared

Data is stored on cloud infrastructure provided by Render (PostgreSQL databases hosted in the United States). We share information only with the service providers needed to operate the product:

  • Render — application hosting and databases.
  • SendGrid — delivery of notification emails.
  • Twilio — delivery of text messages (phone numbers are transmitted to Twilio solely to deliver the message).
  • Vercel — hosting of the web application.

We do not sell personal information. Visitor data belongs to the organization that collected it; that organization acts as the data controller for its visitors and determines retention.

5. Data retention

Visit records, photos, and signed documents are kept while the organization's account is active and are deleted when the organization deletes them or closes its account, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information. Visitors should contact the organization they visited (the data controller). Account holders can contact us directly at support@sentinelskiosk.com and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS), hashed password storage, token-based authentication, and role-based access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using commercially reasonable measures.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
